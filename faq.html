Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services.

How long does picture framing take?

Standard framing usually takes 3-5 business days. For custom premium gold-leaf frames or large orders, it may take up to 7-10 days.

Do you deliver outside Kano?

Currently, we offer door-to-door delivery within Kano. For orders outside Kano, we use trusted courier services at an additional shipping fee.

Can I advertise my business on your site?

Yes! We offer various ad placements including top video ads and middle banner ads. Visit our 'Ads' page to see our weekly and monthly plans.

Do you offer digital web design?

Yes, Clintzy Art's World provides modern web design and branding services for businesses looking to establish a professional online presence.
